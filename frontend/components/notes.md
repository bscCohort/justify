# Components Notes

Importance
- Components are the reusable building blocks of the UI.
- They keep pages small by isolating layout and presentation details.
- Well-designed components make styling and refactors safer and faster.

Major concepts covered here
- Controlled inputs with props and callbacks.
- Presentational components vs. data/logic owners.
- Reusable list items and detail panels.
- Conditional rendering based on props.
